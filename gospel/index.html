The Good News

The Gospel means “Good News.” It is the message that God loves the world and sent His Son, Jesus Christ, to save humanity from sin. Through His life, death, and resurrection, Jesus made a way for people to be forgiven and restored to a relationship with God.

Christianity teaches that all people have sinned and fall short of God's glory, but through faith in Jesus Christ we can receive forgiveness and eternal life.

“For God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish but have eternal life.” — John 3:16

Who Is Jesus?

Jesus Christ is the Son of God and the Savior of the world. Christians believe He lived a perfect life, died on the cross for the sins of humanity, and rose again three days later.

The Message of Salvation

Salvation is the gift of eternal life through Jesus Christ. It cannot be earned by works, but is received through faith and trust in Him.
